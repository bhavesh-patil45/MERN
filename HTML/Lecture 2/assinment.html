-Travelling to Jaipur

Travelling to Jaipur was an unforgettable experience that gave me a glimpse into the rich culture and heritage of Rajasthan. Known as the “Pink City,” Jaipur is full of vibrant colors, magnificent palaces, and bustling markets. Visiting famous landmarks like the Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, and City Palace made me appreciate the beauty of traditional Indian architecture. The local food, especially the Rajasthani thali, added more flavor to the journey. Strolling through Johari Bazaar and Bapu Bazaar was also a delight, with their handicrafts, jewelry, and traditional clothes. -Studying with reels on

Studying with reels playing in the background is something many students can relate to in today’s time. While it feels entertaining and keeps the mood light, it often leads to distraction and loss of focus. Instead of fully concentrating on the books, the mind keeps drifting toward the videos. Sometimes, it even makes studying take longer than expected. Although it may seem fun, I realized that studying without reels or background noise helps me understand and remember things much faster. -About my College Life

My college life has been one of the most memorable phases of my journey so far. It is a time filled with learning, growth, and countless experiences that shaped my personality. From attending lectures and working on projects to spending time with friends in the canteen, every day brought something new. College also gave me opportunities to explore my interests through extracurricular activities, cultural events, and technical fests. The bonds I created with my classmates and teachers will always remain special. Overall, my college life taught me valuable lessons, not just in academics, but also in teamwork, responsibility, and enjoying every moment to the fullest.
